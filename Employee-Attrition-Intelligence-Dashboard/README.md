Employee Attrition Intelligence Dashboard
Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most significant challenges faced by organizations as it directly impacts productivity, hiring costs, and workforce stability.

This project analyzes employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics dataset and develops an interactive Tableau dashboard to identify the major factors influencing employee turnover. The project combines Python-based data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, and business intelligence visualization to generate actionable HR insights.

Objectives
Analyze employee attrition patterns.
Identify key factors contributing to employee turnover.
Develop an interactive Tableau dashboard.
Provide business recommendations to improve employee retention.

Key Business Insights:
Overall Attrition Rate: 16.12%
Sales department has the highest attrition rate (20.63%)
Sales Representatives show the highest attrition (39.76%)
Employees working overtime are nearly 3× more likely to leave.
Frequent business travel is associated with higher attrition.
Lower job satisfaction and poor work-life balance increase employee turnover.
Business Recommendations
Reduce overtime workload.
Improve employee work-life balance.
Strengthen retention strategies in the Sales department.
Enhance employee engagement initiatives.
Improve career growth opportunities.
Implement recognition and reward programs.
